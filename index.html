What is TED ?

TED moves us – driving innovation, sparking curiosity, and reshaping perspectives. It’s more than a platform; it’s a movement where ideas ignite change. A global community, TED welcomes all who seek to understand and inspire. Leading with "Ideas Change Everything"

What is TEDx ?

TEDx events bring TED’s spirit to local communities, blending world-changing ideas with meaningful conversations. Independently organised but TED-inspired

What is TEDxMITWPU ?

MIT World Peace University envisions a world shaped by tradition, innovation, and peace. TEDx fosters change through ideas that inspire, transform and change

THEME
Threads Of Identity

Like a horizon over the sea, identity expands and transforms with time. TEDxMITWPU seeks to uncover hidden facets of selfhood, forging new connections and discoveries.

Balancing our many selves is never easy, yet it is through this very act that we embark on a profound journey of self-discovery.

Identity may never be fully defined—but isn't that the beauty of unravelling it?

SPEAKERS

Samaakshi Jha (Digital Creator)

    A digital creator from Raipur, Samaakshi is known for her expressive reels and relatable content. She blends humor with social commentary, connecting with Gen Z audiences.

    Iconic Moment: Viral reels about student life and social issues.

    TEDx Vibe Match: Her work reflects how digital platforms shape identity, turning everyday stories into threads that define who we are in a hyper-connected world.

Capt. Dharamveer Singh (Motivational speaker & Ret. Army Officer)

    A retired army officer and motivational speaker, Capt. Dharamveer served over 17 years in the Indian Army. He is now an entrepreneur, and shares life lessons rooted in discipline and purpose.

    Iconic Moment: First to report the 1971 Longewala attack; also known for TEDx talks.

    TEDx Vibe Match: His life mirrors the evolution of identity through service, duty, and reinvention—making his story a powerful thread in the fabric of national and personal transformation.

Angkit Joshi (Creator & Speaker)

    An emerging creator and speaker, Angkit is known for introspective content exploring emotions, self-worth, and societal roles. He connects deeply with youth through spoken word and poetry.

    Iconic Moment: Widely shared videos on self-love and emotional vulnerability.

    TEDx Vibe Match: Angkit’s voice weaves the quiet, often-unseen threads of inner identity—encouraging reflection, healing, and self-acceptance in a fast-paced world.
