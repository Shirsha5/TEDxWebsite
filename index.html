What is TED?

TED moves us - driving innovation, sparking curiosity, and reshaping perspectives. It’s more than a platform; it's a movement where ideas ignite change. A global community, TED welcomes all who seek to understand and inspire. Leading with “Ideas Change Everything,” TED fuels conversations that transform the world. A stage, a story, a spark - TED bridges thought and action in boundless ways. Where ideas converge, the future unfolds.

TEDx: Locally Organised, Globally Inspired

TEDx events bring TED’s spirit to local communities, blending world-changing ideas with meaningful conversations. Independently organised but TED-inspired, each event creates a space where thinkers, doers, and changemakers connect.

WHAT MAKES TEDx SPECIAL?

  • Global Platform, Local Voice: Bringing TED’s format to our community
  • Diverse & Impactful: Covering science, tech, art & social change
  • Authentic & Open: Ideas, not promotion–shared freely
  • Live & Recorded: Talks reach beyond the stage through TED’s platforms
  • Engaging & Thought-provoking: A space for innovation, discussion & action

TEDxMITWPU - Building a Strong Identity

MIT World Peace University envisions a world shaped by tradition, innovation, and peace. TEDx fosters change through ideas that inspire, transform and change. Together, TEDxMITWPU brings these values to life–where knowledge meets wisdom, individuality meets impact, and diverse identities weave a shared future.

Through thought-provoking talks and meaningful conversations, TEDxMITWPU ignites curiosity, self-discovery, and global change — one idea at a time.

Theme: Threads of Identity

Like a horizon over the sea, identity expands and transforms with time. TEDxMITWPU seeks to uncover hidden facets of selfhood, forging new connections and discoveries.

Balancing our many selves is never easy, yet it is through this very act that we embark on a profound journey of self-discovery. Identity may never be fully defined—but isn’t that the beauty of unravelling it?

TEDxMITWPU 2023 - Flip the Switch

Theme: An epiphany, a moment of transformation– “Flip The Switch” was about embracing change, taking risks, and rewriting one’s path.

Speaker Highlights

Ajitesh Gupta (Actor & Theater Performer)

Shared his journey in theatre and storytelling.

Highlighted the power of expression in personal transformation.

Encouraged embracing uncertainty to create impact.

Spoke about his evolution from a teacher to a digital education leader.

Showcased how embracing online platforms transformed learning.

Inspired students to take charge of their education.

Dr Vijender Singh Chauhan (Expert in Polity & Governance)

Discussed governance, education, and societal transformation.

Emphasized the role of knowledge in shaping policy.

Urged young minds to participate in civic change.

Malik Gilani (Entrepreneur, Digital Services & Media)

Shared insights on building businesses in the digital era.

Encouraged taking risks and adapting to technological shifts.

Showcased the impact of digital transformation on industries.

Pratishtha Thakur Haritwal (Taxation Officer, Ex-IT Professional)

Spoke about her career shift from IT to public service.

Showed how resilience leads to meaningful impact.

Ratan Lal Dangi, IPS (Inspector General of Police, Raipur)

Reflected on social initiatives and mentoring youth.

Inspired change-makers to take responsibility for society.
