What is TED?

TED moves us - driving innovation, sparking curiosity, and reshaping perspectives. It’s more than a platform; it's a movement where ideas ignite change. A global community, TED welcomes all who seek to understand and inspire. Leading with “Ideas Change Everything,” TED fuels conversations that transform the world. A stage, a story, a spark - TED bridges thought and action in boundless ways. Where ideas converge, the future unfolds.

TEDx: Locally Organised, Globally Inspired

TEDx events bring TED’s spirit to local communities, blending world-changing ideas with meaningful conversations. Independently organised but TED-inspired, each event creates a space where thinkers, doers, and changemakers connect.

WHAT MAKES TEDx SPECIAL?

  • Global Platform, Local Voice: Bringing TED’s format to our community
  • Diverse & Impactful: Covering science, tech, art & social change
  • Authentic & Open: Ideas, not promotion–shared freely
  • Live & Recorded: Talks reach beyond the stage through TED’s platforms
  • Engaging & Thought-provoking: A space for innovation, discussion & action

What is MITWPU?

MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU) is a premier institution dedicated to shaping future leaders by blending traditional Indian values with modern advancements. With a strong emphasis on academic excellence, innovation, and social impact, MIT-WPU nurtures individuals who strive for global peace, harmony, and progress.

Through its interdisciplinary approach and commitment to holistic education, the university empowers students to think critically, lead with purpose, and contribute meaningfully to society.

TEDxMITWPU - Building a Strong Identity

MIT World Peace University envisions a world shaped by tradition, innovation, and peace. TEDx fosters change through ideas that inspire, transform and change. Together, TEDxMITWPU brings these values to life–where knowledge meets wisdom, individuality meets impact, and diverse identities weave a shared future.

Through thought-provoking talks and meaningful conversations, TEDxMITWPU ignites curiosity, self-discovery, and global change — one idea at a time.
